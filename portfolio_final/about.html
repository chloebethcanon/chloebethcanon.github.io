About

General Terms and Conditions

  • For the time being, Canon Esthetics provides services to women only.
  • There are no refunds for services rendered. If the treatment is performed, it must be payed for. I fyou have any issues for any reason, please contact me within 3 days after your appointment date to discuss how I can make things right. Customer satisfaction is always a top priority.

COVID-19 Policy

  • I am fully vaccinated.
  • It is optional to wear a mask through the premises, but please use proper judgement and wear a mask if you haven't been vaccinated.
  • If you show signs of illness, your appointment will be stopped and rescheduled.
  • I follow strict infection control and disinfection protocols. All implements, surfaces and products are either disposed of or disinfected after each client.

Deposit & Payment

  • Both card and cash are accepted.
  • Deposit: At the time of booking, a NON-REFUNDABLE deposit (25% of your total balance) will be required to reserve your appointment. The deposit amount will go towards your final payment.
  • Payment: You have the option to pay-in-full at the time of booking. Otherwise, you may pay the remaining balance at the end of your appointment in cash or by card.
  • Please note: a 3% fee is applied to card payments on the day of your appointment.

Day of Appointment

  • Please do not arrive more than 5 minutes prior to your appointment time. If you do, please wait in your car. If I'm ready early, I let you know.
  • If you are 20 or more minutes late, your appointment will be cancelled with the option to reschedule.
  • If you do not show (or call) on the day of your appointment, you will lose your deposit and be charged an inconvenience fee (50% of remaining balance).
  • I apologize for the inconvenience, but please do not bring children or guests. They will be asked to wait in the car.

Cancellations and Rescheduling

  • If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please do so at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment time.
  • If you cancel within 24 hours of your appointment, you will be charged an inconvenience fee (25% of remaining balance).
