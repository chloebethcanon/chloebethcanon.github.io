Skin Treatment Policies

  • Before booking a facial or back treatment, you will be asked to fill out an online consultation form to explain any skin concerns or conditions which will determine which products may or may not be used.
  • Facials are customized to your specific skin concerns. We can discuss further at the time of your appointment.

Chemical Peel Terms & Conditions

  • Chemical peels are a great way to exfoliate and rejuvenate your skin. Bio-Elements™ chemical peels target the uppermost layer of your skin on a microscopic level, allowing it to be a pain-free experience and requires no down-time.
  • Depending on your skin goals and skin analysis, you may choose to have up to six treatments at 2-4 week intervals. This series of treatments will significantly help with concerns such as hyperpigmentation, acne and wrinkles.
  • You are NOT a candidate for chemical peels if:
    • You have sensitive or reactive skin, Rosacea, Eczema or any other skin disorder.
    • You have waxed anywhere on your face within 48 hours.
    • You have used Accutane in the last 6 months.
    • You are sunburned.
    • You are pregnant or breastfeeding.
    • If you are undergoing chemotherapy, please discuss with your doctor before booking.
  • Before your chemical peel:
    • Stop the use of any skin thinning products, such as topical acne medications, Retinol, Renova, Retin-A or any AHA/BHA exfoliants for at least 48 hours before your peel.
    • Regularly use SPF before your appointment and avoid prolonged sun exposure.
  • After your chemical peel:
    • Wait at least 72 hours before using any exfoliating products.
    • Continue to wear SPF and avoid direct sun exposure.
    • Keep skin as moisturized and hydrated as possible and practice a regular skincare regimen with gentle/sensitive skincare products (cleanser, toner, serum, moisturizer).
